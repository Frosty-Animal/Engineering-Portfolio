Project: Built Racing Drones for Competition

Overview

This for fun project was started by me and acouple of friends who bored at work one day and wanted something new to break the monotony of work and school. We ended up fixating on racing drones as we recently got on to watching several drone racing tournaments and wanted to test our own skills. We ended up even making a club out of it for a while during my sophomore year of college. Each drone took about 10 hours to build and program as well as being a good starting experience on getting good at soldering as there were a lot of parts that needed to be hand soldered... and how to deal with burns as there were a lot of those as well. at top speeds the drones could get over 100MPH and has a battery life of roughly 6 minutes(dependent on how long your drone will be flying at max speeds).
